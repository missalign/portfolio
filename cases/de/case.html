DearEmployee Platform

Redesigned the core workplace assessment experience, reducing friction throughout the survey journey while improving usability, accessibility, and scalability for enterprise customers.

Context

DearEmployee provides organizations with scientifically validated workplace assessments that help companies measure employee wellbeing, psychosocial risks, engagement, and organizational health. The platform transforms employee feedback into actionable insights for HR leaders and management teams.


Challenge

Business

  • Low completion rates
  • Growing enterprise customer base
  • Complex health assessments
  • Different user groups
  • Legacy UI

    • User

  • Surveys felt long
  • Difficult to understand progress
  • Results difficult to interpret
  • HR administrators struggled to configure assessments

    • Discovery & Research

    Key interview insights:

  • Employees wanted reassurance about anonymity
  • HR teams wanted easier campaign management
  • Long surveys would bias the answers towards the end

    • Design Exploration & Delivery

    Impact

    Business

  • Reduced onboarding time by Y%

  • Faster task completion by Z%

    • Team

  • Faster developer implementation
  • Reusable components

    • Reflection

  • Streamlining of constant feedback system with CS
